The challenge

Thousands of thin listing pages. Poor crawl efficiency.

An online marketplace had thousands of listing pages. Most were thin and duplicated. The site had weak topical authority. Crawl budget was being wasted on low-value URLs.

What I did

Templates, schema, and cleaner crawl paths.

  • Built dynamic title tag templates for all listing page types
  • Added product schema at scale using structured data
  • Blocked low-value URLs from crawl with robots.txt and noindex
  • Created category hub pages to build topical authority
  • Improved site speed across listing page templates
The result

171% click growth. 241% more impressions.

Clicks grew from 21.9K to 59.4K in 6 months. Impressions surged from 437K to 1.49 million — a 241% increase. Average position improved from 19.9 to 17.9.
