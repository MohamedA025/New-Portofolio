The challenge

Thousands of pages. Most getting zero clicks.

A global logistics company had a massive site. Thousands of service and location pages. Most had no clicks. Crawl errors were piling up. Metadata was weak or missing. No structured data. No hreflang for their 12 markets.

What I did

Scaled fixes across the whole site.

  • Fixed crawl errors on 5,000+ pages
  • Built hreflang tags for 12 international markets
  • Rewrote meta titles and descriptions at scale
  • Added structured data for services and locations
  • Set up automated Core Web Vitals monitoring
The result

4.85 million clicks in one year.

Total clicks hit 4.85 million. Impressions reached 83 million. CTR held at 5.8%. That's one of the strongest CTR numbers at enterprise scale.
