The challenge

Competing with OTAs on hotel and destination terms.

A luxury hotel group wanted organic bookings. They were losing clicks to OTAs even when ranking above them. Their meta titles were generic. Destination search traffic was untapped.

What I did

Better titles. Better schema. Better targeting.

  • Rewrote meta titles to lead with experience signals, not just the property name
  • Built hotel schema with check-in details, amenities, and price range
  • Targeted destination keywords for each property location
  • Added FAQ schema for common booking questions
  • Improved page speed on key landing pages
The result

184K clicks. 9.65 million impressions.

Organic clicks hit 184K. Impressions reached 9.65 million. Average position settled at 15.3. The schema upgrades drove rich result appearances in search.
