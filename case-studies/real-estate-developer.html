The challenge

Hundreds of property pages. Weak rankings for buying terms.

A top property developer had a large site. Their pages ranked low for high-intent terms. Impressions were strong but clicks were poor. Internal linking between communities was broken.

What I did

Fixed the fundamentals. Built content clusters.

  • Rewrote title tags on 300+ property pages
  • Built location-based keyword clusters per community
  • Added real estate schema with price and availability
  • Improved internal links between community and amenity pages
  • Fixed duplicate content across listing pages
The result

1.48 million clicks. 70.9 million impressions.

Clicks reached 1.48 million. Impressions hit 70.9 million. Average position improved to 17.9. A strong result for a competitive real estate market.
