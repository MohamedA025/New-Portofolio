The challenge

Competing against bigger brands with thin content.

A UK wellness brand had almost no organic traffic. Their content was thin. They were competing against established brands with far more authority.

What I did

Long-tail focus. Schema. Featured snippet targeting.

  • Built a content strategy around long-tail wellness queries
  • Optimised existing pages for featured snippet opportunities
  • Added FAQ schema to 15 key articles
  • Improved page speed and Core Web Vitals scores
  • Built topical clusters around core wellness themes
The result

Impressions up 124%. Clicks up 45% in one quarter.

Impressions grew 124% to 162K. Clicks hit 1,402 with a 45% quarter-on-quarter growth rate. Average position improved to 11.1. A fast start for a new organic channel.
