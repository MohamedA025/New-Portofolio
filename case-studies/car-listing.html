The challenge

Good impressions. Low click-through rate.

A car listing platform had solid impressions but a weak CTR of 3.3%. They ranked for car model terms but were losing clicks to competitors with stronger titles and rich results.

What I did

Better titles. Vehicle schema. Rich results.

  • Rewrote title tags to include price signals and availability
  • Added vehicle schema with make, model, year, and mileage
  • Implemented review schema where eligible
  • Improved mobile UX on listing pages
  • A/B tested title formats to find the highest CTR pattern
The result

CTR jumped from 3.3% to 4.5% in 3 months.

52.9K clicks in just 3 months. CTR improved from 3.3% to 4.5%. Average position moved from 11.1 to 9.7. The schema upgrades drove rich results across vehicle listings.
