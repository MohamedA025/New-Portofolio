The challenge

Good products. Poor rankings.

A jewellery store had strong products but weak search visibility. Average position sat at 14. Most collection pages had no schema and thin copy. Duplicate title tags were hurting crawl efficiency.

What I did

Schema, speed, and stronger content.

  • Added product and collection schema across 200+ pages
  • Rewrote category page copy with target keywords
  • Fixed 40+ duplicate title tags
  • Improved mobile page speed scores
  • Built internal links from blog content to product pages
The result

87.7K clicks. Position halved from 14 to 7.5.

Clicks reached 87.7K over 16 months. Impressions grew 56% to 3.58 million. Average position improved from 14 to 7.5. The schema changes drove rich results in Google Shopping.
