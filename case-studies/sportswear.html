The challenge

Plateaued traffic. Gaps in category coverage.

A sportswear brand had steady traffic but had stopped growing. They were missing seasonal keyword opportunities. Product category pages were thin. Faceted navigation was creating duplicate URLs.

What I did

Seasonal clusters. Cleaner crawl. New landing pages.

  • Mapped seasonal keyword clusters to existing category pages
  • Added keyword-rich content to collection pages
  • Fixed faceted navigation crawl issues with canonical tags
  • Built sport-specific landing pages for key search terms
  • Improved internal linking across product categories
The result

Clicks doubled. Impressions doubled too.

Clicks grew from 37.1K to 81.9K — a 120% increase. Impressions rose from 2.01 million to 4.76 million. Growth held steady across multiple quarters.
