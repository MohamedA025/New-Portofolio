The challenge

Zero history. Zero rankings. Zero trust.

A brand-new store had no organic presence at all. Just 29 clicks in the first month. No rankings, no backlinks, no site authority. Every decision had to be right from day one.

What I did

Right architecture. Right keywords. Right signals.

  • Built a clean site architecture with crawl efficiency in mind
  • Mapped target keywords to every page before writing content
  • Added full schema markup from launch
  • Built initial backlinks through digital PR and supplier partnerships
  • Created a content plan targeting long-tail transactional queries
The result

29 clicks to 5,179 in 6 months.

From 29 clicks to 5,179 in just 6 months. Impressions hit 81.6K. CTR reached 6.3% — a strong number for a new store. The right foundation makes all the difference.
