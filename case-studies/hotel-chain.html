The challenge

Strong brand traffic. Weak non-brand rankings.

An international hotel chain ranked well for their brand name. But non-brand rankings were poor. Amenity and location pages were thin. Internal link flow was weak.

What I did

Expanded local content. Improved site architecture.

  • Expanded location pages with locally relevant content
  • Added FAQ schema for common booking questions
  • Improved internal link flow to high-priority property pages
  • Built content clusters around amenity and experience keywords
  • Fixed thin content on 80+ location pages
The result

166K clicks. A solid 3% CTR.

Clicks reached 166K. Impressions hit 5.56 million. CTR held at 3% — above average for hospitality. Average position improved to 11.3.
